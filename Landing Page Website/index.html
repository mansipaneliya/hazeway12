Frequently Asked Questions

How does the system detect fog? +

Optical scatter sensors continuously monitor atmospheric particle density (visibility) in real-time, activating automatically when visibility drops below safe thresholds.

Does it work in extreme weather? +

Yes. The system is IP67 rated and engineered to operate in temperatures from -40°C to +70°C, including heavy rain, snow, and night conditions.

How do drivers interpret signals? +

We use intuitive traffic psychology: Green (Safe), Amber (Caution), and Red (Danger). No learning curve is required for immediate driver understanding.

Where can it be installed? +

Compatible with standard highway guardrails, gantries, and roadside posts. Solar options allow deployment in off-grid locations.
